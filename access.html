Accessibility

On this page, the importance of accessibility will be explored.

Accessibility principles

  • Perceivable
  • Operable
  • Understandable
  • Robust

Those 4 principles are important as they are the base to make any web page accessible. The principles can be explored on the WebAIM website. They are important as computers are tools that have the potential to be used by everyone

It is very easy to make a website inaccessible without noticing it. Some of the most common errors that people make are low contrat on text, missing alternative text on images and empty form label.

As an example, for the contrast issue the WCAG success criterion 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum) can be used as a ressource to understand the issue. There are also tools to help fix the issue
